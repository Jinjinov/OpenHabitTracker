Take Markdown notes, plan tasks, and track habits — with native apps for Windows, Linux, Android, iOS and macOS, plus a web app. Free, ad-free, and open source. No account needed: all your data stays on your device.

HABITS WITHOUT STREAK GUILT

Instead of a streak counter, every habit has a desired repeat interval. The app shows how overdue each habit is as a percentage of that interval, so you always see what needs attention first. Missing a day never resets anything. Classic streaks are available too — off by default.

NOTES, TASKS AND HABITS IN ONE PLACE

• Notes with full Markdown support
• Tasks with sub-items, priorities and planned dates
• Habits with flexible intervals, timers, repetitions and quantities
• Categories, search, filters and sorting across everything

YOUR DATA STAYS YOURS

• All data is stored locally on your device
• Export and import: Markdown, JSON, YAML, TSV
• Import your notes from Google Keep
• Optional sync: host the free Docker image on your own server and every app, desktop or mobile, can log in and sync

MADE TO FIT YOU

• 26 themes with dark and light mode
• Available in 20 languages
• Keyboard navigation and screen reader support
